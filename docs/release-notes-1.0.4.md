## Full notes

### New
- **Multilingual support** — English, Deutsch, Français, Español, and 日本語. The app follows your device language on first launch; override anytime in Profile → Language.
- **Localized main experience** — Tonight’s GO/MAYBE/STAY IN verdict, target list, sky-profile and 8-direction horizon capture flow, navigation labels, sign-in, filters, and conditions/moon labels.

### Fixed
- **Tab navigation** — Switching between Home, Tonight, Sky, Plan, and Log now always opens the screen at the top instead of preserving scroll position from the previous tab.

### Still English in this release
- Onboarding and full registration copy
- Deep planner drag-and-drop instructions
- Target descriptions and AI-generated difficulty text
- Most equipment form and session log detail text
