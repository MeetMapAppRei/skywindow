# SkyWindow — launch pricing (saved Jun 9, 2026)

Reference for when the app is approved and ready to monetize.

## Model

**Freemium + annual subscription** — best fit because AI horizon analysis, weather, and Supabase/Vercel have ongoing per-user cost.

Optional hybrid later: one-time core purchase + cheaper “Cloud/AI” subscription.

## Recommended launch prices

| Tier | Price |
|------|-------|
| **Pro (launch)** | **$29.99/year** or **$3.99/month** |
| **Pro (mature)** | **$39.99/year** or **$4.99/month** — only after strong reviews and weekly retention |
| **7-day free trial** | Yes, on annual |

## Free tier (enough to hook, clear upgrade path)

- 1 observing site (flat horizon or manual obstruction entry)
- Tonight’s verdict + limited target list
- 1 equipment profile
- No AI horizon scans, or **1 free scan** during onboarding

## Pro (paid)

- AI horizon capture / analysis
- Multiple sky profiles & sites
- Full planner, calendar, sessions
- Notifications (good-night check)
- Unlimited equipment profiles

## Optional tiers (later)

- **Lite** — ~$14.99/year: 2 sky profiles, no AI
- **Hybrid** — $12.99 one-time for core app; $24.99/year “Cloud” for AI + sync + notifications

## Avoid at launch

- **$9.99+/month** — too high for an unproven niche app
- **$50+ lifetime** — risky unless AI usage is capped
- **Fully free with ads** — wrong audience; server costs don’t scale

## Cost sanity check

Each AI horizon capture ≈ a few cents (higher for multi-direction captures). At $30/year, margin is fine if most users scan a site once or twice and reuse the profile. Consider a soft cap (e.g. 12 AI scans/year on Pro) if needed.
